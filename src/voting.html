Why Vote?

Voting for our server helps us gain visibility on Minecraft server lists, bringing in new players and helping our community grow. As a thank you for your support, you'll receive rewards for each vote!

Voting Rewards

  • 5 Amethyst Shards (server currency) per vote
  • Random loot box with a chance for rare items
  • Voting streaks grant additional rewards

Voting FAQ

How often can I vote?

You can vote once per 24 hours on each voting site. With 5 voting sites, that's up to 5 votes per day!

When do I receive my rewards?

Rewards are delivered automatically when you are online. If you vote while offline, you'll receive your rewards the next time you log in.

What is a voting streak?

A voting streak is when you vote every day without missing a day. The longer your streak, the better rewards you'll receive!

What can I do with the rewards?

Amethyst Shards can be used in the server shop to purchase items, upgrades, and special perks. Loot boxes contain random items ranging from common to legendary rarity.
