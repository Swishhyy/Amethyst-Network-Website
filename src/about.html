About Amethyst Network

A vanilla-enhanced Minecraft server with a focus on community and exploration

Our Story

Amethyst Network was founded in 2024 with the goal of creating a welcoming Minecraft community where players can enjoy a near-vanilla experience enhanced with carefully selected plugins. Our server is inspired by the beauty of amethyst geodes in Minecraft, with custom generation of stunning amethyst caves and environments.

Our Philosophy

We believe in maintaining the core Minecraft experience while adding quality-of-life improvements through plugins. We focus on building a respectful community where players of all ages can enjoy building, exploring, and collaborating without excessive rules or restrictions.

Server Specifications

  • Version: Minecraft Java 1.21.4
  • Server Type: Paper (optimized Minecraft server)
  • World Size: 50,000 x 50,000 blocks
  • World Generation: Custom terrain with enhanced amethyst features
  • Player Slots: 50 players
  • Difficulty: Normal

Server Rules

  1. Be respectful to all players
  2. No griefing or stealing
  3. No excessive profanity or inappropriate behavior
  4. No client-side modifications that provide unfair advantages
  5. Respect the natural environment - no mass deforestation without replanting

Full rules can be found on our Discord server. Joining the server means you agree to follow these rules.
